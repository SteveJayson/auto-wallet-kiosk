Cashier Guide

  1. Receive the physical cash from the customer.
  2. Ask for the 3-digit OTP Code on their screen and type it here.
  3. Click VERIFY OTP to pull up their transaction.
  4. (Optional) Upload the GCash proof of transfer and type a quick greeting.
  5. Click SEND TO CUSTOMER SCREEN to finalize!
